Evaluation

Students will work in class on the exercises with guidance from me using the whiteboard to explain and work through the exercises and examples provided. Upon understanding, the students will begin independent practice with us taking breaks from working to review and ensure understanding has been acquired. Students will have the correct answers before leaving class to have problems they can review and study for future reference.
