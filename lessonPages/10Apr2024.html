Fundamental Economic Concepts - Markets

Warm-Up

Economies can be categorized into 4 different types: traditional, market, command, and mixed. Economic decisions are made by different parts of society in different types of economies. Economic decisions can be made by the people of a society, by governments, or by businesses and consumers.
Mark each statement with the correct economy the following choices: traditional, market, command, and mixed.
_______________ The central government plans to produce 2.5 million cars next year.
_______________ The men of an African tribe move their herd of cattle to a better grazing area.
_______________ Consumers in a country are free to choose from limitless goods and services.
_______________ The central government decides to lower taxes on all cars imported into a country.
