Introduction

We are going to review fractions, starting with what they are and how to name them. Fractions are used in many real-world situations. In particular, after our fraction review, we are going to have a unit on measurement and conversion, which will have a lot of opportunity to use fractions.

Today, we are going to be putting fractions on a number line so that we can compare them. So, we'll start with how number lines work, and then look at fractions on a number line.
